How We Work

From Inquiry to Delivery

A proven 8-step sourcing process that ensures transparency, quality, and reliability at every stage.

01

Buyer Inquiry

Submit your sourcing requirements, target specs, and quantity through our RFQ form or direct contact.

02

Requirement Analysis

Our sourcing team reviews your product specifications, compliance needs, quality standards, and delivery timelines.

03

Supplier Matching

We identify verified factory partners from our network that best align with your specific requirements.

04

Sampling & Negotiation

Pre-production samples are developed for buyer approval. Pricing and terms are finalized.

05

Production Monitoring

Dedicated oversight ensures factories adhere to agreed timelines, specifications, and quality benchmarks.

06

Quality Inspection

Multi-point quality control — inline, pre-shipment, and independent third-party inspection available.

07

Packaging & Docs

Professional packaging per buyer requirements, with complete export documentation and certificates.

08

Global Shipment

FCL/LCL container booking, freight forwarding, customs clearance, and final delivery coordination.
